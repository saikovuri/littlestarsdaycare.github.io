Enrollment

We'd love to welcome your family to Little Stars Home Daycare.

🌟 Now Enrolling for 2026!

We welcome families looking for a safe, structured, and caring home daycare environment. Spots are limited to maintain our small-group setting.

How It Works

Our enrollment process is simple and personal. Here's what to expect:

📞

1. Reach Out

Call or email us to introduce your family and discuss your childcare needs and schedule.

🏠

2. Schedule a Tour

Visit our home daycare in person to see the space, meet us, and ask any questions you have.

📋

3. Complete Paperwork

We'll provide enrollment forms, emergency contacts, medical info, and all the details you need.

🎉

4. Welcome Day

Your child begins their Little Stars journey! We offer a gradual transition to make day one smooth and fun.

What We Offer
