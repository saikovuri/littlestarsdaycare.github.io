Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from parents about Little Stars Home Daycare.

What ages do you accept?

We accept children from 18 months through pre-K (age 5). We also offer a Spring Break camp and Summer camp for school-age children ages 3 and up.

What are your hours of operation?

We are open Monday through Friday, 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM. Drop-off begins at 8:30 AM and pick-up is by 5:30 PM.

What should my child bring each day?

Please send your child with:

  • A change of clothes (weather-appropriate)
  • Diapers and wipes (if not potty trained)
  • A comfort item for nap time (nap mat, stuffed animal)
  • Any special medications with written instructions
  • Sunscreen (for outdoor play)
  • Lunch

We provide snacks, and activity supplies!

Do you provide meals and snacks?

We provide a healthy breakfast each morning and a PM snack each afternoon. Parents are asked to send a packed lunch from home. We accommodate food allergies and dietary restrictions — just let us know during enrollment.

What is your sick child policy?

For the health and safety of all children, we ask that sick children stay home. Children should be symptom-free (no fever, vomiting, or diarrhea) for at least 24 hours before returning. See our Policies page for full details.

What is your late pick-up policy?

Pick-up is by 5:30 PM. Late pick-ups may incur a fee after the first 10 minutes. We understand emergencies happen — please call us as soon as possible if you're running late.

How do you handle discipline?

We use positive guidance and redirection. We never use physical punishment. We help children learn to express feelings, resolve conflicts, and make good choices through age-appropriate strategies and lots of patience.

How do I enroll my child?

Start by contacting us to check availability and schedule a tour. After your visit, we'll provide enrollment forms and walk you through everything. Visit our Enrollment page for the full process.

Do you observe holidays?

Yes, we are closed on major holidays including New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving (Thursday & Friday), and Christmas (December 24–25). A full holiday calendar is shared at enrollment and available on our Policies page.

Can I tour the daycare before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage all families to schedule a visit. You'll get to see our space, meet us, ask questions, and get a feel for the environment. Contact us to set up a time.

Still Have Questions?

We're happy to help! Reach out anytime and we'll get back to you quickly.
