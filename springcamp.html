Join us for a fun-filled Spring Camp! Each day has a unique theme packed with crafts, outdoor play, sensory activities, music, and hands-on learning.

Five exciting themed days to keep campers engaged, curious, and having a blast!

Monday 🌿

Nature Day

Spring collages, nature art, sensory bins & weather songs

Tuesday 🌈

Colors Day

Color sorting, sponge painting, rainbow crafts & matching games

Wednesday 🤸

All Kinds of Play

Indoor & outdoor free play, blocks, cars, fine motor toys & dance

Thursday 🐞

Bugs & Garden

Ladybug crafts, insect toys, sensory bins & pretend gardening

Friday 🎉

Fun Party Day

Water play, dance party, free-choice centers & celebration!
