Welcome to Little Stars! 🌟

We provide a nurturing home-based daycare experience focused on learning, creativity, structure, and lots of fun. Our goal is to help children build confidence, independence, and strong social skills in a small-group setting.

🏠

Home-Based Care

A cozy, family-like atmosphere where every child receives personalized attention and feels right at home.

📚

Early Learning

Age-appropriate activities that build literacy, math skills, creativity, and curiosity through hands-on exploration.

🤝

Social Development

Small-group interactions that help children develop communication, empathy, and teamwork skills naturally.

🌿

Outdoor Play

Daily outdoor activities that encourage physical development, gross motor skills, and a love for nature.

🍎

Breakfast & Snacks

A healthy breakfast and afternoon snack provided daily to fuel growing minds and bodies.

💜

Safe & Structured

Consistent daily routines in a secure environment so children — and parents — always feel confident and comfortable.
