Daily Schedule

A balanced mix of learning, play, meals, and rest to keep your child happy and engaged.

Our Typical Day

Each day at Little Stars follows a consistent, age-appropriate routine that gives children security and structure while leaving room for fun and creativity.

8:30 AM
🌅 Arrival & Free play to start the day.
9:00 AM
🍽️ Nutritious Breakfast — A wholesome morning meal with fresh fruits, grains, and kid-friendly favorites to fuel the day ahead.
9:30 AM
📖 Circle Time & Learning Activities — Songs, stories, letters, numbers, and themed lessons.
10:00 AM
🧩 Learning Centers & Small Group Activities — Hands-on exploration and skill-building fun.
10:40 AM
🌳 Outdoor Play — Fresh air, physical activity, and nature exploration.
11:20 AM
🧼 Diaper Change & Restroom — Hygiene and wash-up before lunch.
11:30 AM
🍽️ Lunch — Children enjoy their packed lunch together.
12:00 PM
😴 Quiet Time / Rest — Nap or calm activities to recharge for the afternoon.
1:30 PM
🧼 Diaper Change & Restroom — Fresh and ready for the afternoon.
1:45 PM
🍪 PM Snack — A healthy afternoon snack to refuel.
2:15 PM
🎨 Creative Activities — Arts, crafts, sensory play, and imaginative exploration.
3:00 PM
🌳 Outdoor Play — Fresh air, physical activity, and nature exploration.
3:45 PM
🏠 Indoor Play — Free play, building blocks, puzzles, and imaginative games.
4:30 PM
✏️ Table & Choice Activities — Coloring, playdough, board games, and quiet favorites.
5:00 PM
🧼 Diaper Change & Restroom — Hygiene and wash-up before leaving.
5:15 PM
👋 Clean up & Pick-up — Wind-down time with fun activities until parents arrive by 5:30 PM.

Flexible & Consistent

While we follow a structured routine, we adapt to each child's needs and energy levels throughout the day.

🌦️ *Outdoor activities are weather permitting. In case of rain or extreme temperatures, we substitute with indoor gross-motor play.

🚽 *We constantly encourage children to use the restroom outside of scheduled times as needed. Potty breaks are never restricted to the schedule — every child is supported whenever they need to go.
