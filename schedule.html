Our Typical Day

Each day at Little Stars follows a consistent, age-appropriate routine that gives children security and structure while leaving room for fun and creativity.

⏰ Time 📋 Activity 👶14–24 months 🧒2–3 years 🎒3+ years
9:00 – 9:20 AM 🏠 Arrival & Free Play Soft toys & sensory play Blocks & pretend play Cooperative play & puzzles
9:20 – 10:00 AM 📖 Circle Time Songs & actions Colors & simple concepts Calendar, weather & discussion
10:00 – 10:30 AM 🍽️ Morning Snack Encouraging self-feeding Spoon/fork practice Independent eating & clean-up
10:30 – 11:00 AM 🧩 Learning Centers Stacking & sensory play Sorting & fine motor Pre-writing, STEM & art centers
11:00 – 11:30 AM 🌳 Outdoor Play Walking & push toys Running games & slides Group games & obstacle course
11:30 – 11:45 AM 🧼 Hygiene & Transition Diaper change & books Restroom routine Hand wash & quiet reading
11:45 AM – 12:30 PM 🍽️ Lunch Encouraging self-feeding Eating with guidance Independent eating & helping clean
12:30 – 2:30 PM 😴 Nap / Rest Nap Nap or rest Quiet rest with books & quiet toys
2:30 – 2:45 PM 🧼 Wake Up & Care Diaper change Restroom Restroom & transition
2:45 – 3:05 PM 🍪 Afternoon Snack Self-feeding practice Social snack time Independent snack & conversation
3:05 – 3:30 PM 🎨 Art / Sensory Simple crafts Guided crafts Creative projects
3:30 – 4:15 PM 🏠 Free Play Exploration play Pretend play Social & group play
4:15 – 4:40 PM 📖 Story Time & Music Short stories Interactive stories Story discussion & songs
4:40 – 5:00 PM 👋 Quiet Play & Pick-Up Books & soft toys Puzzles & drawing Calm activities
Includes: 🍽️ Morning snack 🍽️ Lunch (packed) 😴 2-hour nap 🍪 PM snack 🌳 Outdoor play 🧩 Learning centers 🎨 Art & sensory 📖 Story time

Flexible & Consistent

While we follow a structured routine, we adapt to each child's needs and energy levels throughout the day.

🌦️ *Outdoor activities are weather permitting. In case of rain or extreme temperatures, we substitute with indoor gross-motor play.

🚽 *We constantly encourage children to use the restroom outside of scheduled times as needed. Potty breaks are never restricted to the schedule — every child is supported whenever they need to go.
