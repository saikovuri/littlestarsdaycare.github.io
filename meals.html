Meals & Snacks

Delicious, nutritious food prepared fresh daily to fuel growing minds and bodies.

Healthy & Balanced Nutrition

We provide nutritious meals and snacks daily. Our menu includes fresh fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and balanced protein options. We accommodate allergies and dietary needs with parent communication and careful planning.

🥞

Breakfast Options

Whole-grain pancakes, oatmeal, fresh fruit, scrambled eggs, and yogurt parfaits.

🍌

Morning Snack

Sliced fruits, cheese sticks, whole-grain crackers, and veggie sticks with dip.

🥗

Lunch

Grilled chicken wraps, pasta with veggies, turkey sandwiches, and soup with fresh sides.

🍪

Afternoon Snack

Granola bars, apple slices with peanut butter, trail mix, and homemade muffins.

Allergy Friendly 🤝

We work closely with parents to accommodate all food allergies and dietary restrictions. Your child's safety and health always come first.

Our Meal Philosophy

🥦

Fresh Ingredients

We prioritize whole, fresh foods and minimize processed items in every meal we serve.

👩‍🍳

Prepared with Love

Meals are cooked in-house daily so every bite is warm, fresh, and made with care.

🧒

Kid-Approved

We make healthy eating fun by including foods children actually enjoy and look forward to.
