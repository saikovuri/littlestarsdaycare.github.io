Meals & Snacks

Delicious, nutritious food prepared fresh daily to fuel growing minds and bodies.

Breakfast & PM Snack

We provide a healthy breakfast each morning and an afternoon snack. Parents are asked to send a packed lunch with their child. We accommodate allergies and dietary needs with parent communication and careful planning.

🥞

Breakfast

Whole-grain pancakes, oatmeal, fresh fruit, scrambled eggs, yogurt parfaits, and cereal options.

🍪

PM Snack

Granola bars, apple slices with peanut butter, cheese & crackers, trail mix, and homemade muffins.

🍔 Packed Lunch from Home

Please send a nutritious packed lunch with your child each day. We'll make sure they have a comfortable, social lunch time together!

Our Meal Philosophy

🥦

Fresh Ingredients

We prioritize whole, fresh foods and minimize processed items in every meal we serve.

👩‍🍳

Prepared with Love

🧒

Kid-Approved
